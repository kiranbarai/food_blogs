Kolkata Food's

Kolkata, the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal, is famous for its rich and diverse culinary culture. From spicy street food to traditional Bengali cuisine, Kolkata has something to offer for everyone. Here are some of the famous Kolkata foods.


Macher Jhol

Macher jhol is a popular fish curry that is commonly prepared in the eastern states of India, particularly in West Bengal, Assam, and Odisha. The dish is typically made with freshwater fish, such as rohu, catla, or hilsa, and a variety of spices and herbs.

Kosha Mangsho

Kosha Mangsho is a traditional Bengali dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is a slow-cooked spicy mutton curry that is typically served with rice or bread.

Alur Torkari

Alur torkari is a popular Bengali dish made with potatoes and a variety of spices. It is a common vegetarian dish in Kolkata, the capital city of the Indian state of West Bengal.

Mishti Doi

Misti dahi is a beloved dessert in Kolkata, the capital city of the Indian state of West Bengal. Kolkata is known for its rich culinary culture, and misti dahi is a popular sweet dish that is often enjoyed as a dessert after a meal or as a snack.
